If Unity plugin don't work on your Chrome Browser, you have to enable NPAPI plugins or try to launch the game with different browser.

1. In the address bar at the top of the screen, type chrome://flags/#enable-npapi

2. In the window that opens, click the link that says Enable under the Enable NPAPI flag.

3. In the bottom left corner of the page, click the Relaunch Now button.
